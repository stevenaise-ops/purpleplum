Institutions that have selected Zenus as their banking provider typically fall into one of two situations: they have a clear market to serve but no technical team to build on the Zenus infrastructure, or they're evaluating Zenus and need to understand what the end product will look like before committing.

In both cases, Purple Plum provides the integration layer and the customer-facing product — so your team doesn't need to become Zenus API specialists.

What Purple Plum adds to TUUM

TUUM is an exceptional core. What most clients need alongside it is a polished customer product, configurable onboarding, and an operations backoffice. That's exactly what we build.

  • ✓
    White-labeled web and mobile banking front end
  • ✓
    KYC/KYB onboarding journeys connected to Onfido or Sumsub
  • ✓
    Operations and compliance backoffice with CMS
  • ✓
    Card issuance module (optional) via Clowd9 or i2c
  • ✓
    API orchestration layer routing between TUUM and your front end

How it connects

Our orchestration layer consumes TUUM's APIs and translates them into the product experience your customers see. We maintain the TUUM adapter — so when TUUM evolves, your front end doesn't need re-engineering.

Provide your TUUM instance credentials and product configuration. We map them to our front-end modules and configure the CMS. No TUUM expertise required from your side.
